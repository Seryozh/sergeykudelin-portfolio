Recent work

  • FutureClinic Outreach. Cold-outreach engine for doctor-creators on YouTube. Pipeline: channel discovery via ScrapeCreators, state-license verification through custom ScrapFly browser tooling (400× cheaper than the commercial API), Gemini Flash for patient-intent classification of comment threads, Opus for personalized 5-email sequences in the founder’s voice, and a Slack-gated human approval step before send. Reply rates landed near 10%, roughly 5× the typical cold-email baseline. Layered Nikola on top: a Modal-hosted Claude Agent SDK agent with a persistent filesystem brain that reconciles Gmail against the prospect list and runs the daily batch autonomously.
  • FutureClinic Creators. AI content engine that turns a doctor’s YouTube channel into ready-to-record scripts, titles, and thumbnails in their voice. Pipeline: Opus generates a voice profile from transcripts, a weekly SERP cron mines topics scored for patient acquisition rather than viral reach, FAL composites thumbnails, and an agentic Opus loop writes scripts with self-review passes for voice match, hook strength, and CTA placement, plus a banned-phrase linter on the output. Next.js + Supabase, 28-endpoint REST API frozen as a contract for the companion mobile app, 54 tests plus Playwright E2E.
  • LogiScan. Phone camera + Gemini that reads every sticker on a pallet, cross-references the manifest mid-inference, and flags discrepancies in real time. Cut nightly audits from 90 minutes to 10 at ~$0.002 per scan, with no backend and no data retention.
