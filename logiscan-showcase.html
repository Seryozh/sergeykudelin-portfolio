LogiScan

Point your phone at a pallet. Know what’s missing.

I built this solo to kill a nightly warehouse chore: checking every package against a list by hand. LogiScan reads each shipping label with Gemini vision, reconciles it against the manifest mid-inference, and flags any discrepancy. It is a PWA with no backend, costs about $0.002 per scan, and cut a 90-minute audit to about 10. Press scan below.
